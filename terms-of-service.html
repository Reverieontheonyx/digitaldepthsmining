1. Service Description

Digital Depths Mining provides an automated business development communication platform for automotive dealerships. Our service sends follow-up emails and SMS text messages to customers on behalf of dealerships to assist with vehicle inquiries, appointment scheduling, and customer engagement.

2. Consent to Communications

By providing your contact information to a dealership that uses our platform, and by consenting to receive communications, you agree to receive:

  • Automated email messages related to your vehicle inquiry
  • Automated SMS/text messages related to your vehicle inquiry (if you confirm consent via text)

SMS Double Opt-In

Before receiving any marketing text messages, you will receive a single consent request message asking you to confirm. You must reply YES or Y to begin receiving SMS follow-up messages. If you do not reply or reply with anything other than YES or Y, you will not receive further text messages from us.

After confirming, you will receive a confirmation message acknowledging your opt-in. This confirmation, along with your reply, is recorded as your documented consent to receive automated text messages from the dealership through our platform.

Message frequency varies based on the follow-up sequence. You may receive up to 8 messages over a 30-day period.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

3. Opting In and Opting Out

To opt in to SMS: Reply YES or Y to the initial consent request message.

To opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time. Additional opt-out keywords: CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, QUIT, END

To opt out of email: Click the unsubscribe link in any email.

Opt-out requests are processed immediately. You will receive a confirmation of your opt-out.

5. Lead Sources and Consent Obligations

Lead contact information processed through our platform is sourced from one or more of the following:

  • Dealership-provided data: Website inquiries, in-person visits, phone inquiries, and other direct customer interactions
  • Third-party lead providers: Services such as AutoTrader, CarGurus, TrueCar, Cars.com, and similar automotive lead platforms
  • Publicly available records and databases: Information obtained from public filings, government records, and other lawfully accessible public sources

Consent Process for All Lead Sources

Regardless of how contact information is sourced, no marketing text messages are sent until the end-user affirmatively opts in. Every end-user receives a single consent request message that clearly identifies the dealership and describes the type of messages they will receive. Only end-users who reply YES or Y receive any subsequent SMS messages. This opt-in reply is recorded with a timestamp as documented proof of consent.

End-users who do not reply, or who reply with anything other than YES or Y, receive no further text messages.

For Leads From Public Records

Where contact information is sourced from publicly available records, no prior business relationship or consent exists. These consumers receive only a single opt-in request message. No marketing, promotional, or follow-up messages of any kind are sent unless the consumer affirmatively opts in by replying YES or Y.
